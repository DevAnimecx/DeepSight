Introduction

DeepSight is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data: anonymous usage analytics, error logs (no personal info), and feedback you voluntarily provide. We do NOT collect your source code, API keys, or personal information unless you explicitly share it.

How We Use Your Information

To improve the tool, fix bugs, and understand usage patterns. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information. Anonymized statistics may be published for transparency.

Data Security

All processing happens locally on your machine or through encrypted API calls to your configured AI provider.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us for any privacy concerns.

Contact

For privacy concerns, reach out via GitHub Issues or email.

Questions?
